**Intended Workflow:** Customer Fulfillment - Service Recovery.
**Problem Solved:** Standardizes difficult customer conversations, turning a negative experience into a structured choice that might save the sale.

#### 🔄 Iteration Log

**Attempt 1:** "Apologize to a customer because we don't have their item." 
*(Error: The email sounded robotic, insincere, and offered no solution.)*

**Attempt 2:** "Write a friendly apology email to a customer for an out-of-stock item and offer a refund." 
*(Error: Good, but it didn't try to save the sale by offering alternative options.)*

**Attempt 3 (Final Prompt):** "Act as a Customer Success Manager. Write an empathetic, professional email to [Insert Customer Name] regarding Order [Insert Order Number]. The item [Insert Item Name] is currently out of stock. Offer them two clear options: a full immediate refund, or a 15% discount if they choose to wait for the restock on [Insert Date]."

**Automation Potential:** High. Easily integrated into a CRM system.
**Risks & Limitations:** Sending this to a VIP or enterprise client might feel too "automated." 
*Mitigation:* High-value clients should receive a personalized phone call instead of an AI email.
